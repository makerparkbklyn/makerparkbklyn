Mission

Maker Park is a vision for an adaptive reuse park along the Williamsburg waterfront that captures the creative ethos of the neighborhood around it, and reflects its rich history by breathing new life into the site's industrial fabric. The idea springs from an awareness that both our waterfront heritage and the local creative community is being wiped away at alarming speed.

We also believe that parks have the potential to be much more than two-dimensional green spaces.
